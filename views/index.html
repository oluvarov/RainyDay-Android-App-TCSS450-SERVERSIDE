RainyDay is an android app created by team 1 for their TCSS 450 term project.
The app allows users to see the weather forecast for their current location and add friends to chat with.
With RainyDay, users can stay up to date on the latest weather conditions and plan their days accordingly.
Additionally, the app's chat feature allows users to connect with friends and discuss the weather or any other topic they choose.
